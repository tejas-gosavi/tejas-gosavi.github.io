## What is Back of the Envelope Calculations?

It is the hypothetical analysis of resources required to solve a particular problme. Some founders calls it Back of the Tissue and we(software guy's) calls it capacity esimation, there might be more definations to it.

## Why it is needed?

To get idea of scale with which we will be dealing or undertand boundries of the problem.
